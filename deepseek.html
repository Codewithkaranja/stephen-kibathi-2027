Leadership Biography

A Visionary Leader for Kenya's Future

Stephen Kionywe (Kigathi) is a charismatic, diligent, and principled leader known for his unwavering integrity, empathy, and intellectual approach to community development. As the current Chief Whip of the Real Estate Stakeholders Association (RESA), he has demonstrated exceptional ability to unite diverse groups around development-focused leadership.

His leadership journey is rooted in grassroots engagement, with a proven track record of empowering youth, supporting education, championing healthcare access, and advocating for fair housing policies. Stephen combines traditional leadership virtues with innovative, technology-driven solutions appropriate for the digital era.

Through his various initiatives, Stephen has consistently shown dedication to accountability, transparency, and citizen participation—values that form the foundation of his service-oriented leadership philosophy.
