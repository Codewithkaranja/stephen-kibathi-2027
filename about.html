About Hon. Kigathi

Visionary Leader ⭐ Community Champion ⭐ Kigathi for Kikuyu

Biography

Hon. Stephen Kigathi is a dedicated public servant, community organizer, and visionary leader with a proven track record of transformative development in Kikuyu Constituency. Born and raised in the heart of Kikuyu, Stephen has always been deeply connected to the people, challenges, and opportunities that define our community.

With over 15 years of experience in community development, youth empowerment, and public service, Stephen has consistently demonstrated his commitment to creating positive change through innovative solutions and collaborative governance.

His journey from grassroots activism to leadership positions has equipped him with unique insights into the needs of Kikuyu residents and the practical approaches required to address them effectively.

Explore My Journey

Leadership Journey

2005 - 2008

Community Organizing Beginnings

Started as a youth leader in Kikuyu, organizing community clean-ups and youth sports programs. Founded the Kikuyu Youth Empowerment Initiative that reached over 500 young people.

2009 - 2012

Education & Professional Development

Graduated with honors in Political Science and Public Administration from the University of Nairobi. Simultaneously served as Chairman of the Kikuyu Community Development Association.

2013 - 2015

Grassroots Advocacy

Led successful campaigns for improved public services in Kikuyu, including better waste management and upgraded healthcare facilities. Coordinated relief efforts during flooding emergencies.

2016 - 2018

Youth Empowerment Expansion

Established the Kikuyu Skills Development Center, providing vocational training to over 1,200 youth. Launched mentorship programs connecting young people with industry professionals.

2019 - 2021

Policy & Governance

Served as Special Advisor on Youth Affairs to the County Government, influencing policies that benefited over 50,000 young people across the county. Championed digital literacy programs.

2022 - Present

Kikuyu Movement

Launched the Kikuyu 2027 campaign with a comprehensive vision for Kikuyu's development. Building a people-centered movement focused on transformative leadership and community-driven progress.

Core Values & Principles

Integrity

Transparent, accountable leadership with zero tolerance for corruption. Every decision and action is guided by moral principles and the public trust.

Service

Putting community needs first through responsive, accessible leadership. Public office is a privilege to serve, not an entitlement.

Innovation

Creative solutions for modern challenges through technology, research, and forward-thinking approaches to community development.

Empowerment

Building capacity for sustainable growth by equipping residents with skills, resources, and opportunities for self-reliance.

Compassion

Leading with empathy and understanding, ensuring no one is left behind in Kikuyu's development journey.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in public service delivery, infrastructure development, and community programs.
