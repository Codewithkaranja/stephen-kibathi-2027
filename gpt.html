About Stephen Kionywe (Kigathi)

Stephen is a charismatic and diligent community leader with a proven track record of grassroots action, stakeholder engagement, and policy advocacy. As Chief Whip at RESA, he has championed transparent real estate practice and worked with communities to improve access to housing, fair land use, and stakeholder-driven development.
